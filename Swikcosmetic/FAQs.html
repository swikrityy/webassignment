Your Questions Answered

1. What is the return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused and unopened products with proof of purchase.

2. Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes, all our products are 100% cruelty-free and not tested on animals.

3. How can I track my order?

After placing an order, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor its status.

4. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping fees and times may vary.

5. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team through the 'Contact us' page or by emailing support@charme.com.
